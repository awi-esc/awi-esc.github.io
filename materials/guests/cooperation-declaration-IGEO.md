## 1. Existing scientific cooperation

The Earth System Complexity group at AWI (Dr. Alexander Robinson) and the Palaeoclimate and ice-sheet modelling team at IGEO (Dr. Jorge Alvarez-Solas) have a long-standing scientific cooperation in the field of **ice-sheet dynamics and palaeoclimate modelling**, with particular focus on:

- the dynamics, instabilities, and millennial-scale variability of the Greenland and Antarctic Ice Sheets,
- ice-sheet / ocean interactions and the role of subsurface ocean forcing on marine-terminating glaciers,
- abrupt climate events (Dansgaard-Oeschger and Heinrich events) and their interaction with ice-sheet dynamics,
- joint development and application of the Yelmo ice-sheet model and coupled ice-sheet / climate frameworks.

The cooperation has resulted in joint peer-reviewed publications, jointly supervised doctoral students, shared model development, and reciprocal research stays between Potsdam and Madrid.
