## 1. Existing scientific cooperation

The Earth System Complexity group at AWI (Dr. Alexander Robinson) and the Paleoclimate and Climate Modelling group at UCM (Prof. Marisa Montoya) have a long-standing scientific cooperation in the field of **ice-sheet and climate modelling**, with particular focus on:

- the evolution and long-term stability of the Greenland and Antarctic Ice Sheets,
- coupled ice-sheet / climate / sea-level modelling across glacial-interglacial timescales,
- joint development and application of the Yelmo ice-sheet model and related Earth-system-of-intermediate-complexity tools,
- uncertainty quantification and calibration of ice-sheet models against paleo and present-day observations.

The cooperation has resulted in joint peer-reviewed publications, jointly supervised doctoral and master's theses, shared software development, and reciprocal research stays of group members in Potsdam and Madrid.
